School Uniform

The School Uniform is mandatory for all students

Summer Uniform: 1st April to 31st October

Winter Uniform: 1st Nov to 31st March

(Subject to change according to the prevailing climate conditions)

Blue Uniform: Tues, Thurs, Sat

White Uniform: Mon, Wed, Fri

SCHOOL UNIFORM:

SUMMER
Class I to V Boys Shirt- Half/Full Sleeve Tennis Collar light blue check, Half Pants (Shorts) Navy blue and white, Belt, Socks- Navy blue and white (full size), Shoes- White keds and Black shoes with laces
Class VI to XII Boys Shirt- Half/Full Sleeve Tennis Collar light blue check, Full Pants- Navy blue and white. (Jeans not allowed)
Class I to V Girls Shirt-Half/Full Sleeve Tennis Collar light blue check, Skirt Navy blue and white., Belt, Ribbon- Light Blue and white, Socks- Navy blue and white, Shoes- White Keds and Black shoes
Class VI to XII Girls Salwar- White Kamiz- Full Sleeves kamiz with collar and flaps (blue check and white), Ribbon- Light Blue and white, Belt, Socks- Navy blue and white, Shoes- White Keds and Black shoes.
WINTER
Boys Tie-Navy Blue, Pullover or Coat-Navy blue, Full pant V or round shaped for all classes. Navy blue or woollen steel grey
Girls Pullover or Coat-Navy blue, Skirts for I to V, Navy blue or woollen steel grey

Note: No Student is allowed to come to school without proper uniform.

The aim of the Institution is to impart sound education, inculcating among the boys / girls the habit of co-operation, discipline, self confidence, regularity and punctuality.
