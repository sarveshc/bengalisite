Infrastructure

Science Labs

Modern & well-equipped science laboratories each for Physics, Chemistry and Biology which have facilities for experiments & demonstration of various concepts of science.

Computer Lab

The School has its own computer lab and one ICT lab to train the students in progressive technology, School offers computer science and Informatics Practices as a subject to the senior class.

School Library

The School maintain a good collection of all sorts of Encyclopedias, Reference Books, Dictionary, Atlas and many fiction books. Library is Constantly nurtured by new addition arrivals in the publishing areas.

  • Pupils are advised to make proper use of the Library under the guidance of the Librarian.
  • Only one book shall be issued at a time, Reference book and periodicals are not to be issued.
  • Books borrowed from the Library must be returned on the due date.
  • The pupil shall be liable to make good any damage or loss of the book / books borrowed.

School Transport

Students can avail school transport subject to availability of seats Bus fees must be paid regularly. No student is allowed to discontinue the school bus service in the mid-session except under special circumstances which may be approved of by the management in advance. DTC Bus forms should be deposited by 11:00 A.M. on the announced day.

The aim of the Institution is to impart sound education, inculcating among the boys / girls the habit of co-operation, discipline, self confidence, regularity and punctuality.
