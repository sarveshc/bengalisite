About Us

Student’s Absence/ Leave

Leave of absence is granted on a previous written application from parents or guardian but only for serious and genuine reason. Attendance to school on the first day of the next session after the summer, autumn and winter break or any festival is compulsory.

Attendance on working Saturday is compulsory. It is also compulsory to attend school on all events and competition day. Appointment with doctors, specialists etc. must not be arranged during school hours.

It is compulsory for a student who is absent even for a day to bring the leave application signed by the students parent. If the absence is due to a long illness, a doctor fitness certificate is essential, in addition to the leave letter.

The aim of the Institution is to impart sound education, inculcating among the boys / girls the habit of co-operation, discipline, self confidence, regularity and punctuality.
