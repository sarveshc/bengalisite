Discipline, Courtesy, Punctuality & Order

  • All are expected to be in the school by 9:25 A.M. on all working days.
  • Attendance to all extra curricular and academic activities is compulsory.
  • No. Students will leave the class without class-pass.
  • Students will maintain cleanliness inside and outside the class.
  • No child is permitted to go to any party from school.
  • Maintain an atmosphere of silence during class hours.
  • Girls are not allowed to apply nail polish, mehandi, hair colour, wearing jewellery and multiple rings etc. during school hours. Girls will make 2 plaits and wear ribbons.
  • Boys should maintain short hair, will not colour hair or have centre parting of hair, not stone rings or ear rings allowed with the uniform.
  • Students will come in full school uniform. Students not dressed in appropriate uniform will not be allowed in the school.
  • Pupils must take special care of school property and building i.e.,furniture apparatus, switch board, tube light etc. with the understanding that everything in the school belongs to them. Any damage done to the school property must be made good.
  • The students are not allowed to bring any vehicle except bicycle, Scooter,motor cycle and cars are strictly prohibited.
  • The students should behave politely to bring a good name to the school.
  • Excess pocket money should be avoided.
  • Students who commits a breach of rules shall be issued red card. 3 times offender for the same cause shall be strictly dealt with.
  • Students are not allowed to bring mobile phone.
  • The home work set by the teacher must be done carefully and regularly.
  • Money should not be lent or borrowed or articles exchanged.
  • Pupils are forbidden to bring crackers, explosive, holi colours and otherdangerous materials to school.
  • Change of residence & contact number should be notified to the school immediately.
  • Smoking, chewing gum, eating pan masala, betel leaf, gambling, use of narcotics, drugs or any other intoxicant is strictly prohibited and are sufficient reason for rustication of the student.

The aim of the Institution is to impart sound education, inculcating among the boys / girls the habit of co-operation, discipline, self confidence, regularity and punctuality.
